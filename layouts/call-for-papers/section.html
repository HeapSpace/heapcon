Call for papers_

Heapcon brings world renowned speakers to Serbia, and it showcases regional expertise as well.

Why apply?

  • Share your knowledge, your story.

  • Network with 600+ software engineers and tech enthusiasts that will attend the conference.

  • Enjoy the hospitality Serbia is famous for!

Important details

Your talk should be either 25 or 35 or 45 minutes long

Both international and local speakers are very welcome

Heapcon covers conference admission, accommodation and catering for selected speakers

Early submissions are encouraged! Try to stand out by avoiding last-minute application :)

Join great names such are Arun Gupta, Courtney Hemphill, Maximiliano Firtman, Martin Splitt, Michael Ducy, Marianne Worthington, Patrick Kettner, James Thomas, Rafal Leszko, Antonio Cobo, Heather VanCura, Kaz Sato, Katie Koschland, Ted Neward, Dave Snowden, Nikolay Matvienko, Lois Kim, Alex Casalboni and Sumit Gulwani.
